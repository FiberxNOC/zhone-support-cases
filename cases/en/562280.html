Case content

Summary

Due to a customer report, it was detected that the ONU 5302 was not passing an MTU above 1500. It was determined that there was a problem with the LTF5308B-BHB+ module installed on the XGSPON port of the OLT, which was replaced with the HISENSE LTF5308B-BCA+ model.

Problem detail

Due to a customer report, it was detected that ONU 5302 is not passing an MTU of 1500 or higher on V1-16XC xGSpon with SFP+ module XGS-GP-COMBO-SFP+ Hisense LTF5308B-BHB+, which does not transmit packets with an MTU of 1500 or higher.

Resolution

It was determined that there was a problem with the module LTF5308B-BHB+ installed on port XGSPON of the OLT, which was replaced by model HISENSE LTF5308B-BCA+.
