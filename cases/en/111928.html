Problem detail

Intermittencies were reported on connection to OLT MXK-F108 (single card) and services provisioned in this case, a scaling case with Zhone for assistance. Several remote sessions via Teams were conducted with the supplier where tests were performed on the equipment in question. The patchcord was changed, Linkagg removed, and only one uplink port left. The equipment stabilized without making any specific changes after several days. No conclusive diagnosis from Zhone or a definitive solution to the problem was received.

Resolution

For this case, no a solution definitive from the provider was received, the equipment stabilized without changing anything in particular.
