Traffic on the ONT 2466GN in the V1-16XC was not passing OSPF traffic. Several sessions were held with Glen Lee from Zhone, who escalated using Zhone engineering, and we received a new firmware to resolve the issue.

The Zhone engineering department had been working on this escalated case for several months, around February 2025. They provided a firmware that resolved the issue.
