Case content

Summary

Inconveniences with the V1-16XC OLT when provisioning new GPON services. Observed behavior is that the OLT fails to correctly detect and activate the ONU, despite physical infrastructure being operational.

---

Notes

The inconvenience persists when provisioning some ONUs in OLTs in production. The optical budget of the equipment that presents problems has been sent for provisioning.

Problem detail

Events logged in the terminal monitor of the OLT during provisioning:

2026-01-15 13:37:23 GPON[7859]: DISCOVERED serial-number ZNTS03d19d4d EqD=57364 (onu gpon0/9,10)
2026-01-15 13:37:23 GPON[7859]: ONU(gpon0/9,10) ACTIVATION FAIL, reason : Ranging (SN:ZNTS03d19d4d)
2026-01-15 13:37:23 GPON[7859]: ONU(gpon0/9,10) New Registered.(SN:)
2026-01-15 13:37:23 GPON[7859]: No Entry ONU(gpon0/9,10) serial-number
2026-01-15 13:37:43 GPON[7859]: DISCOVERED serial-number ZNTS03d19d4d EqD=57364 (onu gpon0/9,10)
2026-01-15 13:37:43 GPON[7859]: ONU(gpon0/9,10) Alarm LOAMi ON
2026-01-15 13:37:43 GPON[7859]: ONU(gpon0/9,10) ACTIVATION FAIL, reason : ONU alarm when PW authentication (SN:ZNTS03d19d4d)
2026-01-15 13:37:43 GPON[7859]: ONU(gpon0/9,10) Alarm LOAMi OFF
2026-01-15 13:37:43 GPON[7859]: ONU(gpon0/9,10) DEACTIVATION (Reason: LOAMi)
2026-01-15 13:37:53 GPON[7859]: DISCOVERED serial-number ZNTS03d19d4d EqD=57365 (onu gpon0/9,10)
2026-01-15 13:37:53 GPON[7859]: ONU(gpon0/9,10) Alarm LOAMi ON

The reported errors indicate failures during ranging and authentication (LOAMi / PW authentication), preventing the ONU from completing the activation process.

It is worth noting that on the same PON port (gpon0/9) there are multiple active customers connected to the same splitter and over the same fiber infrastructure.

Resolution

The same issue was presented in the laboratory OLT. The requested steps were applied by the provider and resolved in the laboratory OLT; however, the inconvenience persists in the Carolina production OLT.

Details:

1. Inconsistent provisioning of ONT/UN.

The problem occurs in V1-16XC OLTs with SFP LTF5308B-BCA+.
When connecting different models such as 2466GN, 5228XG, and 5302XG, the OLT displays the message: ACTIVATION FAIL, reason: Ranging.

Tests were performed with the following commands:

olt specific-ploam disable ZNTS051d0e7f

olt specific-ploam disable ZNTS051d0ce5

olt specific-ploam enable ZNTS051d0e7f

discover-serial-number stop

discover-serial-number start 5

onu add 7 ZNTS051d0e7f auto-learning

show system

show interface module-info gpon 0/1

shutdown

#wait 3 seconds

no shutdown

olt specific-ploam enable ZNTS051d0ce5

onu add 18 ZNTS051d0ce5 auto-learning

shutdown

no shutdown

olt signal-check enable

olt rogue-onu detect tx-always enable

show run | grep auto-transceiver-list

olt transceiver-type hisense-e
