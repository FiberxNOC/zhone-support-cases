Case content

Summary

Intermittent connectivity issues were reported on the OLT MXK-F108 (1 single card) and for provisioned services on this device. The case was escalated to Zhone for assistance, multiple remote Teams sessions were conducted to stabilize the equipment after several days. No conclusive diagnostic from Zhone nor a definitive solution to the problem was received.

Problem detail

There were intermittent connections on the OLT MXK-F108 (1 single card) and services provisioned with this setup, where Zhone was escalated for assistance. Multiple remote sessions via Teams were conducted with the provider, testing the affected equipment. The equipment stabilized without any specific changes after several days, however, no conclusive diagnostic from Zhone nor a definitive solution to the problem was received.

Resolution

Technical Support Report

The equipment is currently stable. The following actions were taken without achieving a conclusive result:

* Upgrade MXK 1u.
* Upgrade of ONT 2424A1 to 4.2.004
The equipment generally appears to be in good condition, with:
- Optical power on the ONT at -23dbm (OK)
- Temperature OK
- CPU and memory OK
- Voltage OK

Recommendations if the issue is encountered again:

* Have a console present for better debugging in case the inband management is lost.
* Turn off one of the linkagg arms to freeze traffic on a single interface, as the MAC hash validating traffic over links may be causing packets to be dropped occasionally. Example:
> linkagg arm 0 disable
Or, alternatively:

* Perform a local test by connecting a laptop to an uplink port with an SFP electrical connection (e.g., port 1-1-4-0/eth) and configuring a bridge untagged on this port with the problematic VLAN. Then, test connectivity from that point to isolate the issue.
