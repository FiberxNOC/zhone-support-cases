Case content

Summary

An issue was detected with the OLT MXK-COBIAN219 that was preventing SNMP queries for information about ONTS, as Zhone reported. The process associated with SNMP on the OLT presented a blockage and required restarting the OLT to resolve the issue.

Problem detail

Detection revealed that the OLT MXK-COBIAN219 was not allowing SNMP query of information on the ONTS. Zhone indicated the associated process for SNMP on the OLT, which presented a block and required restarting the OLT to solve the issue.

Resolution

Carrying out a detailed analysis, we observed that the SNMP process accountable for communication between the management card and the line cards has ceased functioning.
It requires being restarted. However, unfortunately, in order to initiate this restart, the card needs to be rebooted.
