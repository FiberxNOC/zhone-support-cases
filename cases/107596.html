Contenido del caso

Resumen

Once configured and CloudCheck is enabled, CloudCheck is not showing the 5G SSID even though the ONT webui and the CLI is showing the 5G active. When the VLAN mapping is applied to radio 6 (WL1.1) - DZS5G_SSID1 the 5G SSID does appear in CC, but it is not the expected SSID from radio 5 (WL1.0)- Eladivinowifi-5G. We have reverted the customer back to 7.0.064 which is working as expected until a new version becomes available.
