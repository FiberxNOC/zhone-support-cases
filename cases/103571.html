Detalle del problema

Se detecto funcionamiento anormal en OLT MXK , el equipo debaja de reponder a ping, snmp, telnet y http por momentos, para resolver el problema se reinició OLT pero se escalo con zhone para un post analisis y poder identificar las causas de este funcionamiento anormal,
por parte de zhone sugirieron lo siguiente, se siguieron las recomendaciones de zhone para estabilizar el equipo

This problem is physical, not logical. However, it can be caused by some factors.

1- Badly installed board. Please check that the card is correctly fixed and screwed all the way. If you do not secure it until the end, we may have poor contact with the bus pins and cause the reported problem.

2- HW problem on the LC card (The most common)

3- OLT bus problem. (perform a visual inspection to ensure there are no damaged or damaged pins)

4- HW problem on the M1 card.

Resolución

Se corrigieron los siguientes factore que zhone sugirió revisar:

1- Badly installed board. Please check that the card is correctly fixed and screwed all the way. If you do not secure it until the end, we may have poor contact with the bus pins and cause the reported problem.

2- HW problem on the LC card (The most common)

3- OLT bus problem. (perform a visual inspection to ensure there are no damaged or damaged pins)

4- HW problem on the M1 card.
