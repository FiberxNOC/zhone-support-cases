Case content

Summary

Intermittent management loss is presented in the ONU model 2466GN associated with the V1-16XC, even after applying the recommended firmware S7.0.100.

Problem detail

Despite the firmware update of the ONUs, the issue persists and to recover the management of the ONU, it is necessary to execute the command "onu re-config", which causes a service interruption for each customer event.

---

(No changes made)

Resolution

Received from the provider.

The engineering team requested to add the following command line to the GPON configuration on port GPON.

india65(config-if[gpon0/2])# onu ip-option 1 ip-host 1 ping traceroute
india65(config-if[gpon0/2])# onu ip-option 2 ip-host 1 ping traceroute
