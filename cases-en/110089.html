Case content

Summary

Intermittent BGP traffic was detected on the models LHX5302, 2424, and 2428, specifically on the ONU V1-16XC. This issue was reported by Zhone, who shared specific configuration details for this scenario.

Problem detail

Intermittent BGP traffic was detected on the OLT V1-16XC in models LHX5302, 2424 and 2428. This issue was reported by Zhone who shared specific configuration details for this scenario.

Resolution

During today's remote session, it was confirmed that the issue was gone after modifying the traffic profile as below.
